A good warm up is recommended before any workout. You can do any one of the warm ups below, or a combination of them. Each person requires a different amount of warm up before a workout, so make sure you warm up until you are ready. Don't strain yourself in the warm up, it is not meant to tire you out.

Warm Up 1:

30 Jumping Jacks
10 Touch Downs
10 Side Step
Repeat above 3 sets

Warm Up 2:

30 seconds Jumping Jacks
30 seconds Pendulum
Swings
Repeat above 3 sets

Warm Up 3:

10 Jump touch-downs
20 Alternate toe touch
30 seconds jog in place
Repeat above 3 sets

Warm Up 4:

10 lateral circles
10 Alternate toe touch
30 Jumping Jacks
Repeat above 2 sets

Warm Up 5:

5 floors staircase
30 seconds Pendulum
Swings
Repeat above 2 sets
